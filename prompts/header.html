Choosing a Secondary Color for xperienced.ai

A secondary color complements your primary color and is used for accents, highlights, and to create visual interest. It should support your brand's primary color without overpowering it.

Your Task

Choose a secondary color for xperienced.ai. Consider:

  • How well it complements your chosen primary color.
  • The overall feeling and style you want to create.
  • Where you will use the secondary color (accents, backgrounds, buttons, etc.).
  • Experiment with different secondary colors and see how they look together. Use online color palette tools for inspiration.

Once you've made a decision, let me know your chosen secondary color (e.g., "I choose gray - #6c757d") and we'll move to defining brand typography.
